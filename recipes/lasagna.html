Lasagna Recipe

Classic Lasagna

This classic lasagna recipe is a comforting Italian dish that's perfect for family gatherings. Layers of rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta cheese, and tender pasta sheets are baked to perfection, creating a hearty and satisfying meal. Each slice reveals layers of flavor and texture — tangy tomato, savory meat, and gooey melted cheese — making lasagna a great make-ahead dish that actually tastes even better the next day as the flavors meld.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 2 cloves minced garlic
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, cook ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until meat is no longer pink; drain excess fat.
  3. Add crushed tomatoes, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. In a greased baking dish, layer meat sauce, lasagna noodles, and cheese. Repeat layers.
  5. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake an additional 25 minutes or until bubbly.
  6. Let stand for 10 minutes before serving.
