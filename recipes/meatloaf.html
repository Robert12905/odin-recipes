Meatloaf Recipe

Classic Meatloaf

This classic meatloaf recipe is a hearty and delicious comfort food that's perfect for family dinners. A staple american dish, it combines ground beef with breadcrumbs, milk, and seasonings, baked to perfection and often topped with a ketchup-based glaze. Meatloaf is versatile — swap in ground turkey for a lighter version, add chopped vegetables for texture, or brush on a sweet-savory glaze before baking for a caramelized finish. It pairs well with mashed potatoes and roasted vegetables for a classic homestyle meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds ground beef
  • 1 cup bread crumbs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup ketchup
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine all ingredients.
  3. Transfer the mixture to a greased loaf pan.
  4. Bake for 60 minutes or until the internal temperature reaches 160°F (71°C).
  5. Let it rest for 10 minutes before slicing.
